About Us
eDC is the Entrepreneurial Club of IIT Delhi managed and run by a set of enthusiastic students aiming to create a sustainable and diverse entrepreneurial ecosystem where the young and elderly can come together to take Entrepreneurship a step ahead in India.
Our Vision
Entrepreneurship in India is yet to reveal its hidden potential. It will not only help India to frontier with the world leaders but also unlock the quality of brains that we are so proud of. With this idea, the Entrepreneurship Development Cell of IIT Delhi (eDC) wishes to inculcate and enrich the entrepreneurial environment in India by creating an easily accessible and exhaustive set of resources for the entrepreneurs as well as the students, the budding professionals, mentors, angel investors and the venture capitalists through various fun-filled yet educating sessions such as Startup Showcases, competitions, eTalks and so on and so forth.

Our Programs

Startup 101

Aimed at inspiring people who are new to Entrepreneurship to take it up as a career choice, this program intends to foster the spirit of Entrepreneurship among the student community through memoirs of successful businessmen, panel discussions and case study challenges.

Startup 201

This program is directed towards people who are already into the Startup Bandwagon and intend to start a business of their own. Through a series of 8 lectures, this initiative wishes to enhance the knowledge of these wantrepreneurs. Loosely based on ‘How to start a Startup Course’ by Y Combinator, this program is being held once a week in association with Ivy Cap Ventures.

Founder Diaries

This year, eDC is launching ‘Founder Diaries’, a one of its kind initiatives where seasoned founders who are also alumni of IIT Delhi share their Startup and Product journey with students and early stage Entrepreneurs thus inspiring them to do something big in their own lives.

Startup 301

A mentorship program planned for people having Startups, we intend to help them in their endeavours by bringing together Alumni, Venture Capitalists and Domain Experts on board thereby facilitating their performance.

Innovation Grand Challenge

A Business Challenge for students, we aim to foster their technical skills and capabilities through this competition.
